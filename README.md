# 💡 Key Insights

* 📈 November 2017 recorded the highest sales, profit, and discount
* 📊 Year 2017 showed the highest overall growth
* 📉 February had the lowest sales and profit (low demand period)
* 👥 Consumer segment is the dominant customer group
* 💰 High discounts negatively impact profitability
* ⚠️ Some products generate high sales but low profit
